About Me

Hi, I'm Bonnie Barksdale

I graduated with a degree in Photography and now I'm making my living taking pictures of people and their pets. My business has grown from just taking pictures of dogs and cats to photographing birds, reptiles, fish, and other exotic animals.

Where It All Began

I started out volunteering for the local Humane Society while I was in college, and taking photos of pets up for adoption on their website. That led me to start working with the people who adopted the pets and to photograph these furry friends in their new homes with their adoptive families.

Humane Society

I want to complement and promote the work of the local Humane Society, so 10% of all sales are donated to the Humane Society to help with expenses of fostering pets for adoption.
